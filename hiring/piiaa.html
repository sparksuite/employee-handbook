Proprietary Information and Inventions Assignment Agreement

Like with most other companies, all employees are required to sign a PIIAA contract. Youʼll be asked to do this on or before your start date. Loosely speaking, this type of document accomplishes two things:

  1. It requires you to keep our companyʼs trade secrets confidential.
  2. It assigns ownership of work you create for our company to our company.
